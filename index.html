About Green Environment Consultancy Service PLC


Established in 2010, Green Environment Consultancy Services PLC (GECS PLC) has grown to become one of Ethiopia's most trusted environmental consultancy firms. We are a team of highly qualified professionals specializing in agriculture, environmental protection, solid and liquid waste management, natural resources management, watershed management, forestry, energy, and irrigation.

Our expertise spans over 15 years of combined experience in research institutes, universities, government agencies, and development projects. We are certified Level One by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change to conduct Environmental and Social Impact Assessments (ESIA).

Company Profile

Established in 2010, GECS PLC has grown to become one of Ethiopia's most trusted environmental consultancy firms. Our team of highly qualified professionals specializes in agriculture, environmental protection, waste management, natural resources management, and climate resilience.

We pride ourselves on our comprehensive approach to environmental challenges, combining scientific rigor with practical solutions that benefit both people and planet. Our work spans across government, non-government, and private sector organizations, delivering impactful results that stand the test of time.

As a Level One certified firm by the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, we maintain the highest standards in Environmental and Social Impact Assessments (ESIA) and sustainable development practices.
